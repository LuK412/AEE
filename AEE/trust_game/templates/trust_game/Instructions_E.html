What happens in Part II

In each group, there will be two persons: One Person A and One Person B.

Person A is endowed with {{ Constants.endowment }}.

Person A can send some or all of his {{ Constants.endowment }} to Person B. Before B receives this amount it will be tripled.

Once B receives the tripled amount s/he can decide to send some or all of it back to Person A.
